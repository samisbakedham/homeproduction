4) Entry and Health Notes

  • Argentina: U.S. tourists do not need a visa for stays up to 90 days.
  • Chile: U.S. tourists do not need a visa for stays up to 90 days; a Tourist Card is issued on entry and must be returned on exit.
  • Brazil: U.S. citizens must have a valid visa or e-visa before travel.
  • CDC recommends routine vaccines; Hepatitis A and B are recommended for Argentina and Chile.
  • CDC recommends yellow fever vaccine for Rio de Janeiro; it is not required for entry.

Always confirm entry rules and vaccine guidance close to departure.
