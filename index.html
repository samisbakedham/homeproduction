    Patagonia Break (3 + 3 Nights)

    Day 1 - Fly to El Calafate Check in, shoreline walk, stock up on snacks.
    Day 2 - Perito Moreno Day Full-day glacier visit with boardwalks and viewpoints.
    Day 3 - Buffer + Lago Argentino Flexible day for a boat excursion or rest.
    Day 4 - Transfer to El Chalten Bus transfer and short sunset hike.
    Day 5 - Main Hike Full-day trek (Laguna de los Tres or alternative).
    Day 6 - Secondary Hike Shorter hike + travel prep for Santiago.
    Note: park fees and long-haul flights are not included in daily spend estimates.

    Rio Extension (5 Days)

    Day 1 - Arrive + Sunset Hotel check-in, Copacabana or Ipanema evening walk.
    Day 2 - City Icons Christ the Redeemer early slot + neighborhood stroll.
    Day 3 - Sugarloaf + Beach Cable car in the morning, beach afternoon.
    Day 4 - Culture Day Museum or street art, food markets, live music.
    Day 5 - Pack + Depart Brunch, souvenirs, head to airport.
